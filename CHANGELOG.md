# Changelog

## 1.0.0 - 2026-07-13

- Published the initial synthetic-data quick benchmark with leakage-safe SOC and SOH evaluation.
- Added a complete CPU-oriented SOC comparison: Ridge, Random Forest, Histogram Gradient Boosting, MLP, NARX, causal 1D CNN, GRU, coulomb counting, OCV lookup, and EKF.
- Added three-seed, held-out-cell and chronological held-out-cycle release evaluation plus sensor, temperature, degradation, and load-profile robustness scenarios.
- Added aggregate release artifacts, neural training curves, public-data adapter guidance, expanded leakage tests, and release documentation.

All reported benchmark outputs remain synthetic-data results and are not claims of real-cell performance.
